WEBSITE PRODUCT PACK

Product pack allows you to create the packs of products to enhance your bussiness and attract your customers. Create Combo packs on special occassions and festivals for increasing your sale.

Basic Features

  • Website Product pack allows to create pack of products for website.
  • Special Ribbon is displayed for a Pack Product.
  • Label is displayed for the amount saved by a bundle calculated internally.
  • Details of the products in the pack are displayed in a seperate view.
  • Published products of the bundle act as links to the actual products.
  • Unpublished products appear as readonly and and do not act as a link.
  • Creating Bundle products is very simple as creating the product.
  • Specifying the Properties of Bundle products is very simple.
  • Bundle Products cannot have variants.
  • You can add bundle only, products only or both in the picking while creating Sale Order.
  • Have the seperate option for adding Bundle on creating Sale order.
